Shokunin

A night of alcohol and your inner artist

What is "Shokunin?"

Here's to alcohol: the cause of, and answer to, all of life's problems.
- Matt Groening

Unsatisfied with the stale nighttime entertainment options in Hangzhou, Albert and 小马 banded together to create a new type of event for the restless citizens of the city. Combining their two great loves, alcohol and art, Shokunin was born. They wanted a casual, fun, and unique way to spend time with their existing friends, and for a way to find new ones. Guests arrive at the venue and spend the evening drinking, socializing, and painting with other people with similar interests. Is there any other better way to spend the evening?

What are you waiting for? Grab a friend (or two, or three) and attend a Shokunin event near you! The night is young, and the artistic possibilities are endless.
